Beauty Tips

Your Guide to a Flawless Face

Learn professional makeup techniques and discover how to achieve a smooth, radiant, and long-lasting makeup look with ELCO Face Collection.

Prepare Your Skin

Cleanse, tone, and moisturize your skin before applying makeup for a fresh and healthy-looking complexion.

Prime Perfectly

Apply a lightweight primer to blur pores, smooth skin, and help your makeup last throughout the day.

Choose Your Shade

Match your foundation to your jawline for the most natural finish and seamless skin tone.

Blend Evenly

Blend foundation and concealer using a sponge or brush with gentle tapping motions for an airbrushed effect.

Add Dimension

Use blush, bronzer, contour, and highlighter to enhance your natural facial features beautifully.

Set Your Makeup

Finish with a setting spray to lock your makeup in place and keep your skin looking fresh all day long.
